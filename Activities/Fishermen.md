# Fishermen

### Goal

Filler game, can be started easily and stopped at any time.
Have fun while waiting for an activity to start.

### Description

Two people hold a "fish net", they hold hands and have their arms up.
They secretely decide on a number.
The "fishes" go under the fishing net, as many times as they want, while the fihermen count. (Try to have a flowing stream)
When they arrive at the number they agreed on, they lower their arms and catch some fishes.
These caught fishes become fishermen as well, extending the net.
This goes on until everyone is caught.
