# Opposites Day

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

As opposite as you want or can.
You can reverse the schedule, start the day with lullabies and bringing down the flag, you can have kids plan activities, lead delegation time...

### Instructions

You can chose not to tell the kids at first : you start with the flag up and then put in down, every leader might be dressed, then gradually put on pajamas...

You can also organize activities :

#### Activities

- Reverse capture the flag : try to get caught
- Opposite Talent Show : Show us soething you're bad at
- Opposite sports : football with hands
- Dessert before main course

#### Schedule & Organization

- Start the day by one staff going into the kids room, and asking the kids to get their Leader, they are in the Leaders Meeting room
- We are dressed and ready, and as soon as they get us, we go with them, no questions asked, as if everything was normal
- Then we put down the flag
- We eat breakfast
	- you bring your mouth to the dish and not the other way around
- We make a circle and ask the kids what the activity is. Of course they won't have one, so act surprised but suggest an activity from above
- ...
- The kids put the Leaders to sleep
