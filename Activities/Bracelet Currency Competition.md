# Bracelet Currency Competition

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Leaders have a currency they carry around at all time (bracelets for instance).
They make up rules throughout camp (one every day for a one week camp, the Leader of DOD suggests it).
When this rules isn't respected, the losing Leader gives one bracelet to the one who caught them.

See [[New Day New Rule]] for rules inspiration
