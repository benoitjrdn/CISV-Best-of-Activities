# Get in Clumps

### Goal

Break the ice, find common interests

### Description

The kids mingle, then get in groups with kids that share an interest.

### Instructions

Mingle mingle mingle.
The kids get asked a question by the Leader, and group by their answer.
They can either speak, or act it out (depending if you want more difficulty, or maybe mix depending on the question).

> [!list] Suggestions
> 
> - [ ] What is your favourite
> 	- [ ] colour ?
> 	- [ ] movie ?
> 	- [ ] book ?
> 	- [ ] subject ?
> 	- [ ] plant ?
> 	- [ ] food ?
> 	- [ ] lullaby ?
> 	- [ ] ...
> - [ ] Get in groups by...
> 	- [ ] hair color
> 	- [ ] number of siblings
> 	- [ ] what you do in your free time
> - [ ] Get together in groups of 4 !
> - [ ] ...

### Suggested Debrief

Debrief in little groups (keep the clumps from the last question and send a leader in each).

- [ ] fist to five
- [ ] who did you share the most common interests with ?
