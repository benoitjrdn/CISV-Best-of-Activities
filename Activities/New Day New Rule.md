# New Day New Rule

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Come up with a new rule daily, during Leaders Meeting, that all leaders have to follow.

#### Alternative Versions

[[Bracelet Currency Competition]]

### Instructions

> [!example] Examples of Rules
> - [ ] You can only enter common rooms backwards
> - [ ] You must hop before sitting down
> - [ ] When you use a [specific word] in your sentence, it has to end with "probably"
> - [ ] You must whisper if you say a number
> - [ ] You must take a [french] accent when saying [a specific thing]
> - [ ] You must touch the grass / floor when doing a translation for an activity
> - [ ] You have to act out the verb "run" everytime you use it
> - [ ] You must touch your nose when a kid makes you laugh
> - [ ] [[Get Down Mister President]] -> mister freeze version
> - [ ] You have to say [something] whenever a Leader mentions a fruit or vegetable
> - [ ] If a kid asks you the time, you must give a random animal fact first
> - [ ] When you see someone yawn, you must do an interpretative dance
> - [ ] You must please the last leader standing to sit down ("please X, take a seat"), if they sit before, they can ask for a bracelet
> - [ ] If someone sits, you have to get up instantly
> - [ ] You can't be the last one to sit before asking if you're allowed to
> - [ ] When [specific noise], walk backwards until you touch a wall
> - [ ] When [gesture], everybody drops to the floor
> - [ ] say life juice instead of water
> - [ ] ask for a bite whenever someone eats something red
> - [ ] You must add "according to a legend" after something factual
> - [ ] Fake forget a leader's name once a day
> - [ ] DOD Leader comes up with a secret rule, everybody that gets it during the day wins a bracelet from the bank, everyone else loses one to the bank
> - [ ] When someone mentions the weather, look behind you as if someone called you
> - [ ] Freeze for 5 seconds when ... then act as if nothing happened
> - [ ] ...
