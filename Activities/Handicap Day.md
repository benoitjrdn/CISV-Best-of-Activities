# Handicap Day

### Goal

Realize how hard it is having a handicap.

> [!attention] 
> The kids should not be turning it into a joke, but it is ok for them to have fun and laugh about situations.
> The goal is to realize the struggle and dependency, as long as that's understood, that's good.

### Description

The kids walk around with a handicap for the day : can't see, can't hear, can't walk, missing a hand, two hands. 

#### Alternative Versions

You can also have it implemented in a single activity, like [[Bake a Pizza]].
