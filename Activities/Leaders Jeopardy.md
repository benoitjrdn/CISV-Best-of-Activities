# Leaders Jeopardy

### Goal

Have fun, break the ice, get to know each other, or bond depending on when and how deep.

### Description

Get to know each other by doing a Jeopardy.

You can also prepare a "pre-made" Jeopardy : "who is the most ... ?". If you do, you can come up with dares (but don't tell anyone) : the one who is the most sensitive will have to shave a balloon full of shaving cream, etc etc.

You can also do a Jeopardy with pictures of you as a kid
