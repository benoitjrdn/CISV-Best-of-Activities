# Smugglers

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

It's even more fun in the woods.

Two teams.
The smugglers have to get as many little tiny sheets of paper from `A` to `B`.
They can carry one at a time.

If they get caught, the one who stopped them has three tries to guess where the paper is.

If you were stopped once, you can't be stopped a second time.

### Instructions

One Leader is at point `A`, giving each kid one single tiny paper.
One Leader is at point `B`, receiving and counting them.

See who managed to get the most points.
