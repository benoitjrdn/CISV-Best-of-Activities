# Chicken Monster

### Goal

Have fun, run around.

### Description

Won't fill an entire activity but can be paired with other running games, or on a smaller slot.

Kids are in pairs and hold each others arm. These pairs are everywhere on the pitch.
There is one chicken, chased by one monster.
The chicken can grab the arm of someone in a pair. When this happens, the pair is now a group of three which doesn't work, and the person on the opposite end of the trio becomes the monster, chasing the one who was a monster before and now becomes a chicken.

*Players choose a partner and linking arms they find a space in the playing area. One pair are chosen, one is the mouse and the other the cat. The mouse runs around and can link arms with any person from another pair. The person from the pair who did not get linked to is now the cat and the other is the mouse. The cat has to catch the mouse. If he did, they change roles.*

#### Alternative Versions

In some versions, the kids have to shout like a chicken or run like a monster depending on their role.
Feel free to have multiple monsters and chickens at once.

### Instructions

Don't forget water breaks.
