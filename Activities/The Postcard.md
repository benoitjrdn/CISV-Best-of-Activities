# The Postcard

### Goal

Make the kids understand that speaking a foreign language is nothing normal.
Especially good on camps with a lot of countries speaking the same language.

### Description

In pairs, each kid reads a letter in their native tongue. The other has to write down what they hear, and then read it out loud.

You can make a first part where they write the letter.

### Instructions

Explain this activity in your native language.
Then have all Leaders translate it to their delegation (explain beforehand to the Leaders group).


Do the groups beforehand so you don't pair kids that share a common language.
