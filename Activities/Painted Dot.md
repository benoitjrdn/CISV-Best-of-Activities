# Painted Dot

> [!hint] 
> Go change ! Put an old tshirt, just know you'll get paint on it.

### Goal

Show appreciation

### Description

The Leader reads statements.
You can go paint a dot with your finger on a person if that applies to them.

> [!note] 
> You don't need to have someone for each question.

#### Alternative Versions

You can do it on a sheet of paper if you are in Sweden
