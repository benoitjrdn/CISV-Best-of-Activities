# Red Light, Green Light

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Everyone knows this game.
1, 2, 3, soleil.

The person that is "it" is ~20 meters away from the rest. When he turns his back, people start running towards him, to reach him. He says "green light, red light" and turns around. People instantly freeze. If the person that is "it" sees someone moving, they send them back to the starting line.
