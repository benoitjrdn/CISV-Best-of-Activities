# Great Minds Think Alike

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

#### Version 1

Groups of ~8.
One guesser.
One word to guess.

Everybody writes down a hint. But if two people or more wrote the same thing, they are not allowed to show it.
Once all doubles are out, the guesser has to find out what the original word is.


> [!example] 
> The word is energyzer
> P1 wrote activity
> P2 wrote activitiy as well
> P3 wrote Purple Soup
> P4 wrote energy
> 
> The guesser is only shown word 3 and 4.

#### Version 2

Different game.
They all get a word.
They write 3 words.
The more people wrote the same word, the more points I have.

> [!example] 
> The word is "space".
> I wrote planet, sun, black holes.
> 4 other people wrote planet, 6 wrote sun, no one wrote black hole.
> I get 4+6 points.
