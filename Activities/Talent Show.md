# Talent Show

### Goal

Have fun.
Overcome fear of performing in front of people.

### Description

Just organize a talent show dude.
It can be fun if the Leaders or Staff dress funny and do ratings (only 10s).

### Instructions

Hang a sheet of paper where they can enter how many they are, and how long they need, and if they need materials.
Leaders & JCs should do something too, to encourage the kids to do it too.

Create an area where they can perform.
Enjoy the show.
