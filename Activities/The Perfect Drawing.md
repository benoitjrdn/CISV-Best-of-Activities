# The Perfect Drawing

### Goal

Learn about influence, manipulation, 

### Description

The kids are divided in groups of ~6.
They are prompted to draw [an elephant].
The leader in charge of the group will have drawn [an elephant] themself too, before the start of the activity, and keep it hidden.

The goal of the leaders is to manipulate the kids to draw it as close as their personal drawing. 

Reveal at the end.

#### Alternative Versions

In one group you can prompt the leader to make the group not draw anything at all.

Can be done with creating the perfect city too, if you haven't done 20 activities about the perfect cities already.
