# Wink Murder

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

#### Version 1

In a circle, the group decides of a killer, while the detective is sent away.
That person will wink to others, when they are winked to, they die and lay down.

#### Version 2

Same but walking instead of in a circle.
They can also shake hand and press the hand as the sign instead of winking in this version.
