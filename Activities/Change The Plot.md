# Change The Plot

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

You start by telling a story. Whenever someone says "change", you instantly have to change the plot. There is no limit to how often the people can say "change". 

> [!example] 
> So I was with my sister and we were going to the movies...
> "CHANGE"
> We were going grocery shopping, and I was in the aisle of fresh fruit
> CHANGE
> I was in the aisle of not fresh fruit
> CHANGE
> I was in the aisle of cleaning products, and I bought some white vinegar to clean the mirror of my grandma's bathroom.......
