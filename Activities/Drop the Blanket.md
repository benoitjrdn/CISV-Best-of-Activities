# Drop the Blanket

### Goal

Learn names

### Description

Two teams, one on each side of the blanket.
One person out of each group goes to the front. When the blanket drops, you need to be the first one to say the name of the other person. If you win, they come to your team.

> [!tip] 
> The winner has to say his name too, in case everyone forgot.
