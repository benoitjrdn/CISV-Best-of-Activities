# La Gamelle

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

One person (Leader first round, referred to as "P" for player next) counts (eyes closed) while everybody hides.
Determine one spot, like a flag pole, in an open space. This is the base, and where P counts.
P tries to find everyone and has to move away from the base. 
When finding someone, P runs back to the base and says "I FOUND YOU `NAME`".
Meanwhile, P2 (Player 2) or any other Player can run to the base and free everyone if they reach it before P does.
