# Bombs and Shields

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Mingle mingle mingle.
**Each Player** thinks of one person from the group that is going to be their bomb, and another one who is gonna be their shield.
A timer is started, when it goes off the bomb explodes, and you have to be behind your shield to survive.

So someone's bomb will be someone else's shield, which will make everybody run to and from one another, creating chaos.
Try it with the Leaders, it will make sense.

I don't recommend eliminating anyone if village / YM, play for the sake of fun.
