# I never told you but...

### Goal

Bond

### Description

Can be done plenty of ways.

In a speed dating style for instance, but you can get creative.
You can also make it similarly to the [[Love Bombing Activity]], with one person standing in the middle and everyone saying things, but less suitable for bigger groups.

The goal is to say something positive you think about the person, that you never told them.

*I admire how you are always smiling*
*I love your style*
*I think you are a great singer at lullabies*
*I think you are really good at soccer*
*...*
