# Gala Night

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Gala Night takes place on one of the last day of camp.
The activity is oftentime split into two activities : Gala Night Preparation, where the kids prepare the games, decorations etc and shower and get ready, followed by the actual Gala Night.
Gala Night can have a theme, if mentionned in the precamp (80s party, cultural outfits...).

The older the kids, the more you can let them decide. It's THEIR Gala Night ! Be open to suggestions and encourage them to make propositions.

### Instructions

- Involve the kids as much as possible during preparation.
- Take a lot of pictures during dinnner, group photos, delegation, friends, portraits...
- You can also prepare awards
	- but be mindful to do them well and fun, in a way not to disappoint the kids. Try to make them feel seen, and to mention something they have been working on for instance.
	- *The award for the best singer goes to ...*
- For the music, you can ask each leader to ask for song requests to their delegates.
	- Just be careful to not have the same 3 kids dictate 80% of the requests, try to allow songs only if a lot of people know them.
