# First and Last Impression

### Goal

Take home a nice memory of camp.
Must be done in the very first days of a camp.

### Description

This activity is split into two sessions., one at the very beginning of camp, the second one at the end.

##### First session

In the first one, everybody gets a sheet of paper taped on their back.
People go around and write their first impression of that person (anonymously)

##### Second session

On another sheet / the back of their sheet, do this activity again but this time with the last impression.

#### Alternative Versions

Anonymosity is not a requirement.
You can make a circle with chairs and tape the sheets there, or on a wall or wherever if you don't want people writing on each others back (scandinavians for instance are sometimes very strict with physical touch).
