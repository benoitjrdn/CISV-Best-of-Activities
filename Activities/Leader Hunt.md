# Leader Hunt

### Goal

Have fun, run around, cooperate a little bit.
Compete.
Maybe break the ice, and get to know the names of the leaders if at the beginning of camp.

### Description

The kids are in groups, and have to find every leader, but in a specific order (different for every group). The goal is to collect a signature / sth from every leader, faster than the other teams.

Each leader is somewhere on the campsite.
Once they find a leader, they will have to perform a task, then move on to the next leader.
Fastest team wins (table 1 at dinner, if at the very beginning of camp and no DOD).

*All leaders spread out in the campsite. The group is divided into small teams that have to find
the leaders in a certain order, the order is different in each team (they have a piece of
paper with the names written down). The leaders have tasks for the children to do, before
they receive a signature from the leader to prove that they found him/her.*

You can add Hunters, who can be staffies or JCs, and that can try to catch the kids while they run from one spot to another.
The kids have to hold hands with the whole group, if they don't and get caught, they have to redo an activity.
