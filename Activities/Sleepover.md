# Sleepover

### Goal

Have fun and make the memories of camp last

### Description

At the end of camp.
It makes for great memories of camp.

You can make them bring their mattresses (or if you have those sport mattresses in the activity room it's nice too, to not damage the mattresses) before lullabies.
