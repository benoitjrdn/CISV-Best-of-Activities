# Mirror Mirror on the Wall
Positive Affirmations Mirror

> [!todo] 
> - [ ] Pair up the kids with a Leader
> - [ ] Plan an after
> - [ ] make it anonymous
> - [ ] positivity wall
> - [ ] make a basket in the bathroom and put the things in

### Goal

Build trust in yourself.

### Description

For advanced groups.
The kids will, one by one, go in front of a mirror and say positive things about themselve (as "**you** are a kind person").

We give them prompts.

> [!list] 
> - [ ] What is one thing you like about you ?
> - [ ] What is something about yourself you'd like to change ?
> 	- [ ] Do you think your friends would agree with you ?
> - [ ] What do you think your loved ones like best about you ?
> - [ ] What are the characteristics that people praise you the most for ?
> - [ ] What is one characteristic that you look up to in other people (adults, celebrities, friends...) ?

Then ask them to write positive affirmations from this.
