# Wool Web

### Goal

Show and express love, bond

### Materials

Wool balls. More than you think.

### Description

At the end of camp, make everyone sit in a circle. You say something positive about someone and throw the wool ball at them, while still holding it.
Each person can be picked once.
At the end, there is a huge spider web of how people are connected.

> [!tip] 
> Take a time lapse video

> [!tip] 
> You can cut small pieces of the wool to make bracelets or just to take home
