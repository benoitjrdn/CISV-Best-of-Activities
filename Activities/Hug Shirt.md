# Hug Shirt

### Goal

Bring home a souvenir from camp

### Description

Have (plain white and black) T-shirts (don't buy fast fashion !) and paint (non toxic).

You decide if the kids can pick one, or two, or however many people they want, it's just gonna get messier. Ideally, everyone has one bestie and they get in pairs.
Can be done with the activity buddy too.

You and your duo put paint on your arms, and hug each other, leaving a mark on the shirt. 

New favorite shirt.

> [!tip] 
> Write your name on your T-shirt beforehand !
