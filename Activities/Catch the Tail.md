# Catch the Tail

### Goal

Have fun, run around

### Description

The kids have a tail (a bandana, t-shirt, whatever) tucked in their trousers.
They are alive as long as they have it. Their goal is to snatch other people's tail while not losing theirs.

#### Alternative Versions

Also works with balloons on the ankle that need to be popped.
Can also be run in teams for coop.
