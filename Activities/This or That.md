# This or That

### Goal

Break the ice, get to know each other

### Description

The Leader reads prompts, and the kids, in pairs, answer as fast as they can.

> [!list] 
> - [ ] Pizza or Pasta ?
> - [ ] Lunch or dinner ?
> - [ ] Cats or Dogs?
> - [ ] Summer or Winter?
> - [ ] Beach or Mountains?
> - [ ] Morning or Night?
> - [ ] Sweet or Salty?
> - [ ] Stay Home or Go Out?
> - [ ] Chocolate or Vanilla?
> - [ ] Ice Cream or Cake?
> - [ ] City or Countryside?
> - [ ] Hug or High Five?
