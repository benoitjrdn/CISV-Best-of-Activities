# Frozen Finder

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

All participants close their eyes. A leader touches one person. He is the Frozen one and opens his eyes.
He stands still, and everyone starts walking, eyes closed. When they encoutner someone, they ask ?
Are you frozen ?
They both answer no, except if it's the frozen one.
When you meet the frozen one, he doesn't answer. You then grab his hand and become frozen too.
Game ends when everyone is frozen.
