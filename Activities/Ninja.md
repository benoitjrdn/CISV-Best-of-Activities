# Ninja

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Everybody stands in a circle, a few feet apart. Arms should be able to touch easily.
You have one move only, and you have to touch the arm of one person that is next to you, or get close.
If you hit, that person loses their arm. When both arms are out, you're out.
If you miss, it's their turn.
