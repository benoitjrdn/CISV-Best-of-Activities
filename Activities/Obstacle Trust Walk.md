# Obstacle Trust Walk

### Goal

Develop trust

### Description

Make an obstacle run, the kids are in pairs, one is blindfolded, the other is the guide.
They have to reach point `P` as fast as possible.
Leaders are in the middle and can walk around. If you hit a Leader, you start over.

For more difficulty : you can't touch each other, only talk.

*Divide the participants into couples. One is blindfolded (the car) and the other can see (the driver).Make a big square, in which the cars are standing. The driver has to stay outside the square. One couple acts as police and they have to catch the others. A car is driven, by the driver giving commands to the car (left, right, stop, hurry etc). After a while, they swap positions.*

### Instructions

You need blindfolds, and you need to have the area prepared with a bunch of obstacles (chairs only will do if you have no other materials).
