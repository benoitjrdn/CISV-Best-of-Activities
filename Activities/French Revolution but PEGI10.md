# French Revolution but PEGI10

### Goal

Filler game while waiting for a delayed activity. 

### Description

Make two groups.
The two groups form a line (in a crocodile line), and are facing each other.
They first kid of each group plays rock paper scissors. The winner goes to the back of his line, the loser is sent out.
Last team standing wins.

Works well with Leaders against participants too (hence the name, I made it during a cultural activity).
