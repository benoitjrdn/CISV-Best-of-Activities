# Pick a corner

### Goal

Find common interests
Can be an ice breaker or deeper bonding game depending on the questions.

### Description

"If you were someone from Kung Fu Panda, who would you be ?"

Corner A : Po
Corner B : Tai Lung
Corner C : Oogway
Corner D : Tigress

### Instructions

Come up with a bunch of questions.
Read the question, assign a corner to each option, they then go to that corner.
Ask them to discuss in their groups why they picked that option.

Here are a few example questions, but not the best.

#### Ice breaker examples

> [!list] Q1. What type of vacation do you prefer?
> A: Mountains
> B: Beach
> C: City trip
> D: Forest camping
 
> [!list] Q2. What’s your spirit animal?
> A: Cat
> B: Dog
> C: Bird
> D: Bear

> [!list] Q3. Pick your superpower:
> A: Invisibility
> B: Flying
> C: Telepathy
> D: Time travel

> [!list] Q4. What would you do on a lazy Sunday?
> A: Sleep all day
> B: Watch movies
> C: Go outside
> D: Read or create something

> [!list] Q5. What’s your ideal role in a team?
> A: Leader
> B: Strategist
> C: Supporter
> D: Wild card

> [!list] Q6. Which breakfast food are you?
> A: Pancakes
> B: Eggs & bacon
> C: Fruit bowl
> D: Croissant

#### Bonding examples

> [!list] Q1. How do you handle conflict?  
> A: Avoid it  
> B: Face it head-on  
> C: Try to mediate  
> D: Joke it off

> [!list] Q2. What motivates you most?  
> A: Winning  
> B: Helping others  
> C: Curiosity  
> D: Being different

> [!list] Q3. How do you respond to stress?  
> A: Organize everything  
> B: Talk to someone  
> C: Take time alone  
> D: Keep going until I crash

> [!list] Q4. How do you prefer to learn?  
> A: By doing  
> B: By watching  
> C: By listening  
> D: By reading

> [!list] Q5. What do you value most in a friend?  
> A: Loyalty  
> B: Honesty  
> C: Fun  
> D: Understanding
