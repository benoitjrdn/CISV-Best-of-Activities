# That Word In Between

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

The goal is to try to say the same word.
I say a random word. You say a random word.

You said pencil, I said tree.

Now we say the word that comes to our mind.

Maybe now I say paper pen, and you say idk what. The goal is to win in the least amount of words.
