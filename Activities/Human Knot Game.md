# Human Knot Game

### Goal

Break the ice, have fun

### Description

Everybody makes a tight circle, closes their eyes, and grabs two hands at random.
Open your eyes and undo the knot.
The more people the longer the activity. You can split the group to make it fast.

Ranges from 3-20 minutes, but the longer, the most frustrating it can be.
