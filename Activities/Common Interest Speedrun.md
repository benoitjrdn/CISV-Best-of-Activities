# Common Interest Speedrun

### Goal

Bond, get to know each other

### Materials

Two blankets

### Description

> When the blanket drops, find a common interest with the person in front of you.
> First team to have found a common interest for each member wins. 

Similar setup as [[Drop the Blanket]].
When the blanket drops, the two participants (in each group) have to find a common interest or similarity.
As soon as they find a common interest (that the Leader approves, not too broad), they win and it's the next person's turn.
Faster group to get everyone through wins.

The bigger the number of participants, the more groups.
Aim for groups of 5-10 participants.
Four groups, subdivided in two each, are competing (so 8 groups total).
Group 1 & 2 play together against group 3 & 4, group 5 & 6, and group 7 & 8.
