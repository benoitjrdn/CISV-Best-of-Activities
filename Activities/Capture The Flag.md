# Capture The Flag

### Goal

Have fun, run around

### Description

*Divide the group into two teams. The playing area is divided into two sections, each team having a section. Teams mark a circle (1-2 m) within their section in which they place a flag, this circle is called a safety zone. The aim of the game is to steal the other flag while protecting their own. Players may go anywhere in the area, but if touched in the opposite team’s section they are wounded and must sit still until someone from their own team touches them to revive them. Once reaching the safety zone players can not be wounded but they can only remain in the safety zone for 1 minute and after capturing the flag they must return it to their own safety zone to win.*

> [!tip] 
> If it can be done on a huuuge field it's even better

> [!tip] 
> Can be a great adult-vs-kids activity

#### Alternative Versions

Dirty Capture the Flag : More contact, needs to be caught not just touched, then taken to a prison, and the flags are hidden. Must be played in a big forest. Not suited for younger kids, and you need to put clear limits so no one gets lost.
You can only catch one person at a time, so be strategic and don't let a single person guard the flag.
You win by taking the flag back to your base.
