# Orders of Magnitude

### Goal

Understand that not everything is equal in terms of ecology.
Learn what matters the most, and what is surprisingly high or low.

Can be done with carbon footprint or with water usage.

### Description

The kids get different items, and have to guess which are more polluting.

### Instructions

Split into groups of ~5.
Hand out cards for each group.
They have to get them in order of what is more polluting.

Then reveal.

### Suggested Debrief

Explain that CO2 is used as a proxy, but is not the only way of measuring the impact, and that things are way more complex.

What is not taken into account ? (rare ressources, pollution in terms of plastics, trash, etc).

What surprised you the most ?
