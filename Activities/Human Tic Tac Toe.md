# Human Tic Tac Toe

### Goal

Have fun, cooperate as a group.

### Description

You know how Tic Tac Toe works.
Same but bigger.
Have a big Tic Tac Toe grid 30 meters away from the teams.

#### Alternative Versions

Add as many of these as you want

- The kids have to answer a question about CISV / something before going to put their cross / dot
- They can or can not place their cross / dot on top of the other team
- There can be a parcours before
- They have to win a rock paper scissors before going
