# Cats and Dogs

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

The kids (cats) put one of their shoes in the middle of a circle. They have to go get their shoe, while some other kids (dogs) protect them.

#### Alternative Version

Make a bigger circle, they can only be a few at once, the leaders are the dogs, and they can only be caught if they have a shoe in their hand. So it makes them cooperate, throwing the shoes to each other.
In this version you can get someone elses shoes.

> [!warning] 
> You need to be outside but the kids will also be missing a shoe lol.
> Except if they have an extra pair or slippers.
