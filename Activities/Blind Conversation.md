# Blind Conversation

### Goal

Bond or start opening up, depending on the level of the questions.

### Description

Blindfolded, you don't know who you are paired with.
You talk with your partner.
You can use questions of the [[36 Questions to fall in love]].

You can also, for more privacy, have questions where you only press the hand once for yes and twice for no.

Depending on the level of intimacy of the questions, can be an ice breaker or a bonding / trust game.

In the end, ask if they want to reveal their identity. If both say yes, you can remove their blindfolds.

### Suggested questions

> [!question] 
> - [ ] What do you like best about camp ?
> - [ ] When was the last time you cried ?
> - [ ] Tell me about one of the times you laughed the hardest ?
> - [ ] What is something people don't always see about you, but you wished they did ?
> - [ ] What is your weirdest interest ?
> - [ ] ...

### Suggested Debrief

- [ ] Did not knowing who you were talking to have an impact on how you responded ?
- [ ] Did you find out during the game who you were talking to ?
	- [ ] How did that impact your next answers ?
- [ ] Did you decide to reveal at the end to remove the blindfolds ?
	- [ ] Was it because you wanted to know who it was that was saying those things, or because you wanted to know to who you were talking about yourself ?
