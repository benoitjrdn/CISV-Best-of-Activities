# Zombie Apocalypse

### Goal

Have fun, cooperate.

### Description

The kids are in a secured city. Their goal is to find a cure for the zombies, hidden on campsite, and to duplicate it in a lab. The lab is hidden as well (don't let the Zombies know either).

There are a few Zombies outside, who can only walk. Zombies follow the person closest to them (so they CAN be distracted and not block the lab's entry for instance).
If they catch someone, the person becomes a Zombie as well.

As soon as the cure is found, then the zombies can start running (so if the kids are smart, they will look for the lab first.

The kids have won if they entered the lab with the cure.
