# 3 minutes presentation

### Goal

Get to know each other.
This activity forces to talk about everything you care about and is a great kickstarter for bonding. Should be performed early on camp.
Works extremely well for adult groups for quick bonding.

### Description

The participants, assigned in pairs, talk for 3 minutes straight (each) about as many things about themselves as possible (hobbies, family, pets...).
Once they both talked for 3 minutes, they then go into bigger groups, and introduce their partner to the group, trying to recall as many items as possible.

### Instructions

- split the participants in groups of 6/8/10/12, the more people the longer the activity
- inside those groups, pair the kids
	- you can choose to randomly assign the kids in pairs, or if you can, think of which kids might become friends but don't dare talking to each other yet
	- letting them choose a partner is not recommended, as it might lead to exclusion, and kids might go see people they already know a little
- once they all have a partner, explain the rules, and put a timer
	- the timer is an indicator, feel free to up the time if they keep talking

#### Hints

> [!hint] 
> you can make a "cheat sheet" for questions to ask "favourite colour, pets..."

### Suggested Debrief

No debrief needed.
Rating appreciated (e.g. fist to five).
