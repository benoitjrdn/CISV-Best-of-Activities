# Bake a Pizza

### Goal

Have fun, do something more plastic

### Description

Make pizzas in little groups.
Enjoy them at night in front of a [[Movie Night|nice movie]], during a [[Pyjama Party]].

#### Alternative Versions

The kids can be handicapped :

One has their ears shut, one is blind, one has no hands, one can't talk, one can only move when carried. Great replacement to [[Handicap Day]].

You can make it after [[Cookies Economics]] and in the same conditions, to see if they apply their knowledge. But then have a backup for dinner.
