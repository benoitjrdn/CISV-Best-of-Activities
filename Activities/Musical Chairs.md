# Musical Chairs

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

There are $n-1$ chairs in a circle facing outwards.
Music is playing, and the prople go around the circle (no staying where you are, no overtaking).
As soon as the music stops, you have to find a seat.
The one who can't is out.
Remove a chair and restart.
