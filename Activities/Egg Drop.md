# Egg Drop

### Goal

Come up with strategies

### Description

The kids have materials, and an egg.
The goal is to protect it from a fall, because you will drop the construction from as high as you can.
They win if the egg didn't break.

You can be creative with the materials, and how to get them.
I recommend using something that would've gone to trash anyways.
Gather it for a couple of days prior.
