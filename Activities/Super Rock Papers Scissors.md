### Goal

Have fun

### Description

*Two teams line up facing each other, about two meters apart. Each team has decided weather they will be paper, scissors or rock and on the count of three they show their choice. The team that wins chases the losers and if they tag any before they reach their safety line they are out. The winning team is the one that eliminates the opposition*
