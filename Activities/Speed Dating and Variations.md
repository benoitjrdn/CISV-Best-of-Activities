# Speed Dating and Variations

### Goal

Get to know everyone

### Description

Form an inner and outer circle facing each other.
In a speed dating style, each kid is in front of another kid.
They get prompts from the organizing Leader e.g. "What is one thing you're an expert on ?". After 30 seconds or a minute, the outer group rotates to the right, and they get another question.

#### Alternative Versions - Group presentation

You can do that, and then, each kid gets up one by one, and the other kids try to recall something they said.
"Oh, his favourite animal is a cheetah !"

#### Alternative Versions - Place Mats

Can be used for creating placemats : you draw on someone's placemat then rotate.
"What country are you from"
"What is your favourite color"

### Instructions

> [!question] Suggested questions
> - [ ] what are you an expert on ?
> - [ ] What’s something you do that always makes you happy ?
> - [ ] If you could be invisible for a day, what would you do ?
> - [ ] What’s a food that you weirdly like ?
> - [ ] What do you think your friends like most about you ?
> - [ ] Who do you miss right now ?
> - [ ] What’s something you’ve been scared to try but want to ?
> - [ ] What’s something about yourself that people don’t always see ?
> - [ ] What is something you thought about the person in front of you that you were too scared tell them ?
> - [ ] What is gonna be your biggest regret from camp ?
> 	- [ ] Can you do something to prevent it from happening ?
