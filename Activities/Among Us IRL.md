# Among Us IRL

> [!info]
> Can't really be done in big groups.
> Very campsite-dependant.

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Inspired by the online game.

### Instructions

Everyone gets a paper.
Crewmates have tasks written on it, Impostors have fake tasks to blend in.

There are stations everywhere on campsite (spread out !), monitored by a Leader.

Kirmess style stations (knocking off cans, put pen in a bottle...)

When a crewmate does a task, the Leader at the station crosses it off. When they are done, the Leader in the emergency room collects them. They win if everyone did their task.

If the campsite allows it, the impostors can go everywhere and the crewmates are more restricted.

Once per kid, you can call for an emergency meeting. Leaders will have to commmunicate and send the kids to the meeting. They can vote someone out.

Impostors can kill anyone who is alone.
