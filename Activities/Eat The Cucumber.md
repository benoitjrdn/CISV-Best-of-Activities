# Eat The Cucumber

### Goal

Have fun, bond.

> [!warning] 
> Kind of gross (super gross actually) so adults only, except if the group is **reeeeally** down.

### Description

Form a super tight circle, with one person in he middle, guessing where the cucumber is.
The others are passing it in their back, and try to eat it completely before the person guesses right.
