# Millenium Development Goals Blueprint

### Goal

Learn how to overcome obstacles and not getting discouraged, using stoicism and the method of premeditatio malorum.
Encourage to become a gloabl active citizen.

### Description

The kids learn about and reinvent the Millenium Development Goals.
They then decide on actionable steps to arrive to those goals.
They reflect on how realistic their expectations are, and how to make their goals happen.

### Instructions

Briefly teach the participants about the 8 Millenium Development Goals.
Split them into groups.

#### Part 1

Ask them what they would change : remove, add, or specify.
They can write them down or organize however they want to.
The goal is to have a goal they actually want to achieve.

You can choose one goal or a few / all of them.

#### Part 2

As a group, they decide on how to achieve these goals.
The steps must be somehow actionable.

#### Part 3

Ask them what could go wrong in these actionable steps.
Be realistic, which sometimes implies being a bit harsh.

#### Part 4

Now they know all the ways their possible plan might fail.
Now ask them to establish a plan on how to overcome these possible "fails".
