# Kids Olympics

### Goal

Have fun, enjoy the weather

### Description

Tons of little stations where the kids compete.
Can be done for a whole activity, or an elongated activity, or even on two activities / whole afternoon. 

You need a lot of materials.

### Instructions

Assign a Leader to each station.
Make groups and let the kids choose a group name.

> [!list] Suggested Activities
> - Balance the Egg : They have a spoon in their mouth and hold an egg. They have to do a race / parcours.
> - Flour Apple : Put an apple in a container filled with water. They have to grab it with their mouth. As soon as they managed to 
> - Jumping Sacks : Races in trash bags
