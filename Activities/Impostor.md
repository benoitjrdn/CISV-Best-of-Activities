# Impostor

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

Everybody gets the same word whispered in their ear by the Leader.
Except one that gets "impostor" whispered.
They all say a single word, one by one, that reminds them of the word.
Once everybody said their word, they vote on who they think the impostor was.
