# The Killer's game

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

One supervisor, not playing, tells everyone who their target is, and how to kill them.
They keep track of it.
Last one standing is the winner.
If someone kills someone else, they inherit their target.
If someone understands who their killer is and how they are trying to kill them, the killer dies.

> [!example] 
> `Leader 1` must kill `Staff 1` by bringing them to broom the floor
> `Staff 1` must kill `Leader 2` by slow dancing with them.
> `Leader 2` must kill `Leader 3` by making them teach you how to do a handstand
> `Leader 3` must kill `Staff 2` by making them hold food during 10 seconds
> ...
> ... make `player` name 5 colours in less than a minute
> ... make `player` give you a piggyback ride
> ... make `player` give you a shoulder massage 
> ... convince `player` to lend their shoes
> ... convince `player` to help you bind your shoes
> ... have `player` say your full governmental name
> ... have `player` spell CAMP out loud
> ... have `player` compliment your elbows
> ... get `player` to hum the star wars theme
> ... get `player` to give you a ring temporarily
