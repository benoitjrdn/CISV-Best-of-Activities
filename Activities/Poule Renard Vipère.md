# Poule Renard Vipère

> [!todo] 
> You need something to differentiate the teams, else it's gonna be a mess to see who is who.

### Goal

Have fun, run around

### Description

"Chicken, Fox, Snake"

Chickens eat the Snakes but are eaten by the Foxes
Foxes are bitten by Snakes but can eat Chickens.
Snakes are Eaten by the Chickens, but kill the Foxes.

All have their home somewhere on the pitch. They cannot be eaten there.
They have to catch all of the one team (see above).

Once caught, you get to the base of the opponent, where you can be freed if someone from your team comes get you.

If you catch someone, you can't be caught while taking the person back.

### Instructions

Have them put on coloured shirts or something so you know who is who
