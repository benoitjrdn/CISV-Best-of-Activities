# ABC fruit

### Goal

Filler game while waiting for a delayed activity. 

### Description

In a circle 1st player names a [fruit] with A, second with B...

It's super super quick to launch, and can be done while already in a circle.

They can say it in their own language as well, and have it translated to the group. Could even be in your native language only. Get creative.
