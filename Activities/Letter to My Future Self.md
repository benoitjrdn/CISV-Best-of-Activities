# Letter to My Future Self

### Goal

Self-development

### Description

The kids write a letter to themself, to open in 10 years (or 1, or 5, or when they're 18).

Bonus point if you take a picture of it and send it as a programmed email for in 10 years. For that you'll need the parent's email (especially at villages and youth meetings) because no way they'll have the same email adress at 11 and 21.
