# Top Ten the game

### Goal

Have fun

### Description

One person is the guesser, the rest get a random (hidden) number.
If they are 10 players, they get a number between 1 and 10.
Then, the Guesser asks a prompt :

> [!example] 
> "A spot to spend the whole day"
> "Act out someone getting a bad present but not wanting to hurt the person who gave it to you, from "I can't hide my emotions" to "I'm the best actor".

Then the persons will respond according to their number, if you are the 10, you will be totally extra, if you are the 1 then pick the worst option.

The Guesser has to guess who was 1, 2, 3 etc.
