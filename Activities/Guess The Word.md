# Guess The Word

### Goal

Have fun, be creative

### Description

> Storytelling with a Mandatory Word

The participants get a secret word assigned by the Leader. They then have to create a short story with this word in it.
The goal of the other participants is to discover what word was the one that was mandatory.

### Instructions

Split the kids into smaller groups.
The more specific the word, the better.
