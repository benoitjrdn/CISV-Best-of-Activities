# Fast Fashion

### Goal

Educate about the consequences of fast fashion for the employees.

### Description

> [!wip] 
> Réfléchir à des incentives pour les riches d'acheter les origamis.

The kids are split into rich and poor.
The poors are gonna be working to do some origamis, while the rich are gonna be buying them.

### Instructions

Split the kids into rich and poor. Don't tell them that of course, and don't tell them about what the other group is gonna do.
Bunch of poors and just a few rich.

The poors are gonna do origamis. They have to reach a certain quota / minute.
One part of the poors are gonna be the "runners" : they take **one** origami at a time and bring it to the marketplace, at the opposite side of the campsite, where the rich will be buying them.

They have to run until they reach a quota, then they can get a 20 seconds break. They can drink during this part, but from a shared glass of water.
No break if they were too slow.

There are way more origami makers than runners. So the origamis accumulate, but that's ok.

The origami makers that are too slow become the runners.

### Suggested Debrief

Dump the huge pile of origamis in front of them to show them the waste.

Maybe watch a video about fast fashion, working conditions and landfills.
